Braces

Total Dental Care provides traditional braces, a reliable orthodontic treatment designed to fix misaligned teeth and bite issues. Our braces are available in a variety of options including metal, ceramic, and clear aligners. They apply gentle pressure to shift teeth into the proper alignment, which results in improved oral health and a more beautiful smile.

Our experienced orthodontists create personalized treatment plans to address each patient's unique needs, ensuring optimal results and lasting confidence. We guide our patients through their braces journey with comfort and support, offering regular progress check-ups, so they can achieve the smile they've always wanted. Invest in your dental well-being today and visit Total Dental Care for expert orthodontic care tailored to your needs.

Invisalign

Invisalign is a discreet orthodontic treatment for having straighter teeth. Total Dental Care offers high-quality custom-made clear aligners that help you to gently shift teeth into the correct position over time.

Invisalign aligners are transparent opposite to traditional braces, and are easily removable for eating, brushing, and flossing, ensuring comfort and aesthetics. Invisalign is a good option for various types of dental issues, such as crowding, spacing, and bite misalignments.

With regular progress check-ups, to Total Dental Care, you will get effective and predictable results within the prescribed time. Visit Total Dental Care to receive the guidance for your Invisalign journey, and get back your confident smile.

Pediatric Dentistry

Visiting a Dental Hospital is usually a nightmare for children. So the Dental team in our clinic is always cautious about maintaining a friendly approach to make the children comfortable in their entire dental procedure. We suggest children's dental examinations should be scheduled at least every six months. During their visit, our paediatric dentist or dental hygienist will clean your child's teeth and take oral X-rays after a thorough examination of the teeth. They will advise the young children on the best brushing and flossing practices for them so that children can start their best dental habits from a very young age.

Total Dental Care strive to deliver the highest quality and comfort to every patient. For our young patients, we give high priority to healthy teeth development and cavity prevention through proper dental hygiene and a nutritional diet. Our kids-friendly hospital environment and kind interactions with kids create positive dental experiences and lifelong healthy dental habits. Visit Total Dental Care for compassionate pediatric dental care promoting dental health and children's confidence.
